Short description

Constanța is a vibrant blend of ancient history and coastal charm on the Black Sea. Its revitalized Old Town invites visitors to wander through narrow streets filled with chic cafes near Ovid Square. The city’s crown jewel, the iconic Art Nouveau Casino, has been stunningly restored, finally unveiling its majestic façade after years of renovation. Just steps away on the promenade, the historic Aquarium continues to delight visitors with its diverse marine exhibits. Together, these landmarks create a captivating waterfront experience defined by architectural beauty and maritime heritage.
